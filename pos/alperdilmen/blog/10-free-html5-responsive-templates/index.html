10 Free HTML5 Responsive Templates

There are times we all felt the need to boost our inspiration and get our creative juices flowing again. Especially when you are under pressure and you must finish a project as soon as possible. Thankfully, there are many talented web designers and developers out there who have the kindness to create neat HTML5 and CSS3 website templates and share them for free with me and you.

In this post, we gathered 10 high quality, free HTML5 templates from around the web, to help you speed up your design and development process and boost your creativity. All these templates are fully responsive and loaded with many awesome features. Feel free to download and edit them!

Enjoy!

1. SportHere Template

SportHere is a Responsive HTML/CSS template with clean structure and beautiful minimalistic design. It can be used for both your personal and commercial projects.

Visit Download Page

2. Frame – Responsive Portfolio HTML5 Template

A simple yet professional HTML5 template from MediaLoot. Ideal for creatives who need a clean portfolio site. Feel free to download and use this item for both personal and commercial projects with attribution back to MediaLoot.

Visit Download Page

3. Piccolo – Bootstrap HTML Template

Piccolo is a free HTML5responsive template from MediaLoot compatible Bootstrap. It has a simple and clean design, with a light subtle grunge background. The theme includes 19 different HTML page layouts with sliders, sortable galleries, lightbox popups, alerts, icons and much more.

Visit Download Page

4. Butterfly – Free Bootstrap Theme

Perfect for any kind of business, portfolios or start-ups. Create a distinctive website that will attract everyone’s attention with this modern HTML/CSS template.

Visit Download Page

5. Elegant – Free HTML/PSD Template

Clean, elegant and to the point! The perfect template for your online portfolio. Created by Diogo Dantas.

Visit Download Page

6. Halcyon Days – HTML Template

A pixel-perfect one page template that uses the latest web technologies, created by Peter Finlan. Great for portfolios, start-ups and  any kinds of business.

Visit Download Page

7. Oleose – Mobile App Landing Page

An eye catching fully responsive HTML5 landing page template. It is compatible with Bootstrap 3 and it comes in three different skins. Perfect to showcase your app.

Visit Download Page

8. Bootstrap 3 Landing Page Template

A simple, easy to customize landing page for your app. It is fully responsive and it includes Sign Up and Sign In pages.

Visit Download Page

9. Landed – HTML5 Template

The perfect template to impress your visitor. This template has a very unique design, it uses awesome parallax scrolling effects and it comes with many layout options.

Visit Download Page

10. Knight – Free Bootstrap Template

A beautifully designed, multi-purpose one page template. This template is 100% responsive, retina ready and fully customizable.

Visit Download Page
