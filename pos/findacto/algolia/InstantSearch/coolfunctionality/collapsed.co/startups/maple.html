Maple

Food delivery pairing top chefs with technology and logistics

Overview
Product Description

Maple was a food delivery startup backed by David Chang that operated in the rapidly growing scene of food delivery services in New York City. Maple did not deliver from any specific restaurant, but instead – similar to Sprig or Spoonrocket in San Francisco – promised restaurant quality meals from a menu developed by big name chefs.
