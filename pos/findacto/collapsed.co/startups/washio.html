Washio

Laundry service that picks up and returns clothing

Overview
Product Description

Washio was an on-demand laundry service. Users would schedule a time and pick-up location for their laundry within a 30 minute window. The finished clothes would then be delivered to them on a return date of their choice, on which date they also would to pay for Wash.io’s services.
