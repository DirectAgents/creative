Fuhu

Children's tablet maker

Overview
Product Description

Fuhu Inc. was a designer and seller of cloud-served software, apps, and services dedicated to creating deep and engaging user experiences on a range of consumer devices for children. Fuhu's most well known product was its Nabi tablet, an Android-based tablet for kids. The device included parental controls and educational games that lined up with elementary school curriculum requirements.
