Tilt

Easily collect money with friends or a community

Overview
Product Description

Tilt was an early pioneer in peer-to-peer money transfers. It was geared more toward group payments than individual transactions, but in some ways competed with PayPal’s Venmo and Square Cash. It was also used for crowdfunding anything from “activities” to “causes,” which made it an alternative to fast-growing GoFundMe.
