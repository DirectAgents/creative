Yik Yak

An anonymous mobile social media application

Overview
Product Description

Yik Yak was an app that allowed people to anonymously create and view discussion threads within a 5-mile radius (termed "Yaks" by the application).  All users had the ability to contribute to the stream by writing, responding, and "voting up" or "voting down" (liking or disliking) yaks.
