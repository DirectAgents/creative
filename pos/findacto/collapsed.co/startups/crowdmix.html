Crowdmix

A social music discovery app based in London

Overview
Product Description

The Crowdmix app promised to ‘enable users to develop and enjoy rich, social media experiences by joining crowds, or communities, of like-minded people, using music as the link’. However despite having 160 staff in its London office and others in its LA office - a staff 10 times the size Spotify had a decade ago - it still never managed to get a full product out the door.
